our history

Khushi International began its journey with a simple yet powerful vision: to bridge the gap between fresh produce and the needs of households and businesses. Starting as a small-scale distributor, the company was founded on the principles of quality, reliability, and community support. With a deep understanding of the importance of fresh vegetables and fruits in daily life, we partnered with local farmers to bring farm-fresh produce to markets. Our commitment to maintaining freshness, ensuring timely delivery, and building lasting relationships with clients quickly earned us a reputation for excellence. Over time, Khushi International expanded its network, embracing innovation and customer-centric practices to meet the growing demands of the catering and retail sectors.
